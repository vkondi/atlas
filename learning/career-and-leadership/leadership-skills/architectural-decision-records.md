# Architectural Decision Records (ADRs)

Documenting architectural compromises, rationales, and context systematically.

## Core Concepts

- **ADR Schemas**: Context, Decisions, Status parameters, and Consequences.
- **Historical Context**: Providing context on design choices for future developers.
