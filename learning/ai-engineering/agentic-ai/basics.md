# Agentic AI Principles

Designing models capable of planning steps, invoking actions, and reacting to errors.

## Core Concepts

- **Agent Loops**: Reasoning loops (Thought-Action-Observation) replacing basic prompts.
- **State Isolation**: Maintaining run states across loop updates.
